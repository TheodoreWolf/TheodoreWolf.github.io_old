Hello, I am a Machine Learning Engineer with a background in Physics and Chemistry. I am very interested in applications of ML to science, particularly to the problem of climate change.

I am currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer at Carbon Re, a startup that uses machine learning to help heavy industry companies reduce their carbon footprint.

Experience

Machine Learning Engineer

Carbon Re, 2022-Present

Working on a variety of projects, including:

  • I was tasked with making ML models based on high dimensional industrial time series. Most of the focus is on cement data which involves complex physical interactions.
  • The work also involves maintaining a complex production environment. The pipeline consists of many steps: requesting live data from an API or MQTT (depending on the client), preprocessing the data with AWS lambdas, storing it in AWS timestream. This data is then fed to the inference service which loads ML models from WandB and sends the results to the online web-platform for the clients to view.
